RECOMMENDATIONS

BMI Focus

  • Maintain a BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 for optimal health.
  • Focus on balanced diet and regular physical activity to manage BMI.
  • Consult with a healthcare provider if BMI is above or below recommended range.

Exercise and Lifestyle

  • Incorporate both aerobic and strength training exercises.
  • Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise per week.

Nutrition and Diet

  • Follow a calorie-controlled, nutrient-rich diet.
  • Focus on vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.

VUMPIT's Remarks

  • Ensure consistent monitoring of BMI and adjust your lifestyle accordingly.
